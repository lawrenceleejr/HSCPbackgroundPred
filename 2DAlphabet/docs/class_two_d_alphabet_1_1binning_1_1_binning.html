Create the RooRealVars representing the X and Y axes.

For the X axis, three RooRealVars are returned in a dictionary with each key mapping the RooRealVar to either LOW, SIG, or HIGH categories.

Parameters

ydict"Y" subsection of the "BINNING" section of the config.

tuple (0) dict of X axis RooRealVars and (1) Y axis RooRealVar.
